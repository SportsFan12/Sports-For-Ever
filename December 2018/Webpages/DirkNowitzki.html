How good was Dirk Nowitzki Really?

Written by Marion Veloria (28/12/18)

Dirk was a constant fixture in the all star team appearing in 13 as well as being selected in 12 all NBA teams. His stats and accolades are up there with the best but his crowning achievement is definitely the 2011 NBA finals where he was able to capture that elusive NBA title he had been so close to getting before in 2006.

Before the 2011 NBA finals Dirk among NBA circles was considered a superstar but not an all time great because of his disappointing performances in the playoffs. And maybe this was an unfair knock on his leagcy but many greats before him were subjected to the same treatment (Charles Barkley, Patrick Ewing and more) so Dirk would not be judged different. However that championship propelled him to higher heights and is now considered by many the best european player to ever play in the NBA and is arguably top 50 all time, which says a lot given the number of all time great players that have played in the NBA.
