Is Jason Garrett the right man for Dallas?

Written by Marion Veloria (29/12/18)

Personally I don't believe he is, he's 76-59 in the regular season and 1-2 in the postseason. Those results don't scream super bowl caliber head coach. We have such a great young team that we need a strong leader like Jimmy Johnson did for the Cowboys in the 90's. Now maybe Jason proves me wrong this upcoming postseason but I doubt it.

So now comes the tough question, who should replace him. Many will say that Kris Richard the current Dallas defensive backs coach who's helped establish a young up and coming defense. But in my opinion they must do whatever they can to get Jim Harbaugh from Michigan or Lincon Riley from Oklahoma. My preference would be Jim Harbaugh because he's had NFL experience with the 49ers and did a great job going to two NFC championship games and a superbowl.
