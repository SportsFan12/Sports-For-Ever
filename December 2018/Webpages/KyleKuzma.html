Could Kyle Kuzma become a superstar?

Written by Marion Veloria (27/12/18)

Kyle had a suprisingly impressive rookie season where he averaged 16 points and 6 rebounds per game. Most importantly he showed a fearlesness to take and make big shots in clutch moments, which to me speaks volumes since there's a bevy of players who don't have this same fearlesness. Physically he looks the part as well standing at 6'9 or 6'10 coupled with decent athleticism and a great shooting touch.

With all this I still don't think he will become a superstar however he doesn't need to be in order for the Lakers to become champions once again. They have Lebron James and another possible superstar free agent coming in 2019 hopefully. Maybe all the lakers need is for Kyle Kuzma to become a solid third scoring option. My prediction is he becomes a border line all star in his peak, which would be considered a success since he was a late first round pick.
