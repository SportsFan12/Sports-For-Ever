Why Tom Brady is the undeniable GOAT

Written by Marion Veloria (27/12/18)

For me this was settled at superbowl 51. That 25 point comback was enough for me to crown Brady as the official GOAT of the NFL. To start of with stats he's third all time in passing touchdowns and fourth all time in passing yards and game winning drives. He has five superbowls with 4 super bowl MVP's, he's been to 8 superbowls, 14 probowls and 5 all-pro teams (first or second).

His stats are great and GOAT worthy but what sepeartes him for me is his overall impact on the NFL world. His comebacks in the fourth quarter have been nothing short of spectacular. He is by no means perfect but more often than not in the biggest momments he always puts his team over the finish line. As I alluded to before his performance in Super Bowl 51 was immense. It shook the entire NFL world and he is arguably the most talked about NFL player in history. 25 point leads use to be an automatic win but now because of Tom we no longer believe that. Maybe I am biased because I am a Patriots fan but what he has done in terms of stats, wins and impact on the game is why I have him as the GOAT.
