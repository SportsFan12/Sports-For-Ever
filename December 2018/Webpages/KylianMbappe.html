Kylian Mbappe, the next superstar

Written by Marion Veloria (27/12/18)

When he burst onto the scene in Monaco I had a feeling that Kylian would be special. But what he has accomplished since then for PSG and France has far exceeded my expectations. He's a world cup winner and was by no means a spectator often an integral part to France's success in the 2018 world cup. And even this season has already scored 16 goals in 19 games for PSG.

Kylian has the makings of the next superstar from sensational athleticism and supreme technical ability. The sky is really the limit for him and in my opinion he is up next. What I mean by this is Messi and Ronaldo have held the crown for the best player in the world for some time (since 2008) and it seems as if Kylian is ready to take that crown in the next couple of years. Maybe a new rising star emerges to challenge Kylian Mbappe but for now the best bet is for Kylian to take the throne.
