Is Rashford a future world class player?

Written by Marion Veloria (31/12/18)

After witnessing Macrus play against Bournemouth today and with his recent form since Ole has come to the club I am more convinced that Marcus will one day become one of the world's best strikers. Barcelona's reported interest in him only cements this case as they look for a potential Suarez replacement as the former Liverpool striker comes towards his twilight years.

This season Marcus has 6 assists already this season, which ties the amont he had all together the past three seasons. To add onto it he has 6 goals that will in no doubt increase thorughout the remaining season. He showed flashes of superstar potential for England and in previous seasons with United but now with the right attacking minded coach in Ole he will now be fully able to fufil that potential. My final verdict is that he will become the superstar we all thought he could be when he burst onto the scene for Van Gaal.
