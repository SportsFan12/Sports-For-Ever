Luka Doncic, how good can he ultimately become?

Written by Marion Veloria (27/12/18)

I remember seeing Luka Doncic hit a step back game winner for Real Madrid and not being all too impressed. Maybe it's just my euroleague bias not saying that the euroleague hasn't produced great players, which it has but I can't deny that when it comes to european players it's hard to see them as future superstars. It's safe to say now though that Luka Doncic may have erased this bias that I hold and I am sure other people feel as well.

He's currently averaging 19.5 points per game along with 6 rebounds and 5 assists per game. Which are incredible numbers for any player but for a rookie it is sensational. What makes him even better is that he doesn't seem scared of the moment as evident in his takeover performance against the Rockets where he outdueled James Harden in the clutch. Ultimately I believe Luka Doncic is a superstar in the making and could potentially one day be the best player in the NBA.
