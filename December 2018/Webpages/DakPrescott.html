Is Dak Prescott a Super Bowl Caliber QB?

Written by Marion Veloria (27/12/18)

In terms of stats it's hard to make a case that Dak Prescott is a superstar quarterback, however to be a superbowl winning quarterback you don't neccasarily need to be one. Dan Marino, Jim Kelly and many more superstar quarterbacks didn't win a super bowl. Instead with no offense intended Trent Dilfer won a super bowl.

What I am trying to say is to be a superbowl winning quarterback is more about the situation you're in to be perfectly honest. You need a great team around you even Joe Montana and Tom Brady were surrounded by great teamates. I believe with Zeke and this young great defense the Cowboys have will allow Dak to contend for Super Bowls. Ultimately however it will come down to him as a playoff run will undoubtedly have signature moments. Meaning he will be faced with adversity at some point and he has to make the clutch play. Wether or not he will do it we don't know but to conlcude I believe he does have the ability to do it and am confident that with this great team he can win a Super Bowl.
