Jose Mourinho sacked! What happens next?

Written by Marion Veloria (27/12/18)

Jose Mourinho has had a great career. Titles in Spain, Portugal, England and Italy. Theres no doubt he's a hall of fame coach and arguably top 5 all time which is no small feat. I remeber watching him in his first Chelsea stint and his treble winning side of Inter and thinking to myself this man might be the greatest coach ever when it's all said and done. However failed stints in Real Madrid, Chelsea (second stint) and now Manchester United have put a dent in that claim.

So what's next for him? A reunion at Inter and Porto would be the logical answer with some actually believing that he could land at Real Madrid once again. The international route has also been made available with some hinting at a Portugal or USA job open for him. In my opinion I believe he needs a break. Regroup so that when he comes back wherever and whenever it may be it will be the old Mourinho. The Mourinho that inspired his players and was a serial winner.
